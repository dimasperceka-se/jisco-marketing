Build a full marketing website for "JISCo" (Journal on Interdisciplinary Science and Community) — an academic publication platform similar to IEEE.org.

---

## DESIGN DIRECTION

Aesthetic: Editorial-academic with a modern scientific precision feel. Think: clean authority, not cold bureaucracy. Inspired by high-end scientific journals + editorial magazines like Nature or MIT Press.

- Color palette: Deep navy (#0A1628) as dominant, crisp white, and a sharp gold/amber accent (#E8A020) for highlights. Avoid purple gradients entirely.
- Typography: Use Google Fonts — "Playfair Display" for headings (authoritative, scholarly), "Source Serif 4" for body text (readable, academic), "JetBrains Mono" for metadata/labels.
- Motion: Subtle entrance animations on scroll (fade + slight translateY). Smooth hover states on cards. No excessive animations.
- Layout: Magazine-grid layout for articles. Asymmetric hero. Grid-breaking pull-quotes.

### 2. HERO SECTION
- Large editorial headline: "Advancing Knowledge Across Disciplines"
- Subtext about the journal's mission

- Background: Dark navy with subtle geometric grid pattern (CSS only)
- Stats bar below hero: e.g. "1,200+ Published Articles | 48 Countries | 300+ Peer Reviewers | Impact Factor 2.8"

### 3. FEATURED ARTICLES
- Grid of 3 featured research article cards
- Each card: discipline tag (e.g. "Environmental Science"), article title, author name, abstract excerpt (2 lines), publication date, "Read More" button
- Use placeholder/sample data

### 4. JOURNAL SCOPE & FOCUS AREAS
- Section title: "Interdisciplinary Research Domains"
- Icon grid (6 focus areas): Environmental Science, Public Health, Social Engineering, Technology & Society, Agricultural Innovation, Community Development
- Use SVG icons or Unicode symbols styled with CSS

### 5. SUBMISSION PROCESS
- 4-step horizontal timeline: Submit → Peer Review → Revision → Publication
- Each step has icon, title, short description

### 6. LATEST ISSUES
- 3 journal issue cards showing volume/issue number, publication date, cover color (CSS gradient), article count
- "View All Issues" button

### 7. EDITORIAL BOARD PREVIEW
- 3 editorial board member cards: name, title, institution, area of expertise
- Clean card design with gold left-border accent

### 8. CALL TO ACTION / AUTHOR GUIDELINES
- Split section: left = "For Authors" (submission guidelines link, manuscript template), right = "For Reviewers" (reviewer registration)
- Dark background section

### 9. FOOTER
- Logo + mission statement
- Links: About, Scope, Ethics Policy, Open Access Policy, Contact
